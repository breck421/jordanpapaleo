Marie

Marie is a Colorado native whose interest of hair developed as a little girl. Coming from a family of 5 girls, Marie was always playing beauty salon and experimented on her sisters. After numerous years of college, she decided to further her passion and attended the Aveda Institute of Denver in 2009. Her love for new people and desire for doing beautiful hair brought her to work at The Station Hair Studio.

Through her dedication, Marie has broadened her career through continued education and has had extensive training from Redken, Pravana, Swartzkopf, Brazilian Blowout, Bumble and Bumble, Moroccan oil and has started training in the DevaCurl. With expertise in cutting and coloring, Marie loves creating multi-dimensional color along with a long layered look.
